Wayne Cordrey Web Portfolio

Updated June 2016

Design is not limited to web pages, or media. Its everywhere, for bettter or worse. My goal is to balance aesthetics and usability. I enjoy the psychology of good user interface design- its great when a site works not just as a matter of function, but communicates well and inspires interaction. Color and texture, action and interaction, smooth responsiveness and scaleability are all essential components of my web designs. Whether I'm working with a designer, layering design into a client's sketch, or working within limits to develop page mechanics, I welcome the responsibility to develop clean, articulate designs and communicate fully during the process. Web designs are by nature collaborations, blending artistic sensibility with usability, while still providing clean functionallity, maintainability and scaleability. Working to make these aspects flow is what I find so deeply satisfying about front end design.
